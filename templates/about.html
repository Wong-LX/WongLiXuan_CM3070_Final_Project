About Fake News Detector

This application was built to fulfil the requirements of the CM3070 module.
Its goal is to help users determine whether an article is misinformation or not.
If an article is determined to be misinformation, the application will further predict whether the article was written to be satire or not.

To use, copy the text of an article and paste it into the text box in the main page.
After which, submit your query by clicking the "Check article" button.
